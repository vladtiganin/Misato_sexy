Misato Katsuragi is one of the main characters in the iconic anime series Neon Genesis Evangelion. She serves as the operations director at NERV, overseeing the battles against the mysterious beings known as Angels. Despite her professional role, Misato is a complex and deeply human character—charismatic, caring, but also burdened by trauma from her past. Known for her lively personality, love of beer, and messy apartment, Misato becomes a guardian to the series' protagonist, Shinji Ikari, offering him both emotional support and tough love. Her relationship with Shinji and other characters reveals her struggles with vulnerability, responsibility, and the weight of her own unresolved pain. Misato’s character is a blend of strength and fragility, making her one of the most memorable and relatable figures in anime.
